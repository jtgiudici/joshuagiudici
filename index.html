About Me

My name is Joshua Giudici.

Design is Paramount. I consider myself an artist first. I am constantly thinking on the creative side of things, viewing the world through creative glasses. I surround myself with creative endeavors at all times. I play guitar, I'm an improv actor, I carry a sketch/notebook, I'm a music junkie and a movie addict. I truly believe this fuels good design/development in my work. The creative process doesn't only exist at the office. It isn't dependent on a desk and a cup of coffee. Creativity and inspiration strikes all day. The difference for me is that I actually write down the ideas the come to me in the shower and put them into action.

Plays Well With Developers. I'm not going to produce a bunch of photoshop mockups and hand them over to a developer to build. Those days are long behind me. Good riddance. Instead, I prefer do the front-end development myself, and I've been doing so for quite some time. There's just way to much that gets lost in translation once you hand your design over to someone else to publish. That's why I work side by side with developers and code directly in to the framework, whether it be Ruby on Rails or something else I have to learn. No problem. I make my own commits and take responsibility for my work. That said, I love working on a team of smart, innovative, and creative people. In fact I thrive on it and believe in the mantra that the whole is greater than the sum of it's parts.

A Student of Life. I'm just not content with what I already know. I'm not happy being boxed in as a photoshop designer, or a mobile designer, or print designer, or whatever. Of course, I am all of those things, but my educational pursuits span much farther than that. I love to learn new things in all areas of my life. Personal growth is very important to me, which is why you'll often find me taking up another hobby or skill. Learning is highly motivating and above all, it's fun. (I'd love to see my grade school teachers faces after reading that nugget)

That's me.
