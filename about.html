About Me

Design is Paramount

I'm an artist first and constantly think on the creative side of things. I surround myself with creative endeavors, absorbing an practicing creative mediums. Passions like these fuel great design and development. I feel that the creative process doesn't begin and end at the office. It is an ongoing process, and creativity strikes all day long. The difference for me is that I actually write down the ideas that come to me in the shower and put them into action.

Plays Well With Developers.

Handing over photoshop mocks is not my style. Instead, I prefer do the front-end development myself. I work side by side with developers and code directly in to the framework, such as Ruby on Rails. I make my own commits to the repo and take responsibility for my work. That said, I love working on a team of smart, innovative, and creative people. In fact I thrive on it and believe in the mantra that the whole is greater than the sum of it's parts.

A Student of Life

I'm just not content with what I already know. I'm not happy being boxed in as a photoshop designer, or a mobile designer, or print designer, or whatever. Of course, I am all of those things, but my educational pursuits span much farther than that. I love to learn new things in all areas of my life. Personal growth is very important to me, which is why you'll often find me taking up another hobby or skill. Learning is highly motivating and above all, it's fun. (I'd love to see my grade school teachers faces after reading that nugget)
