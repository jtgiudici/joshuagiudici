Adziply

Adziply is an online tool for small business owners to easily build successful Facebook ad campaigns. The short-term goal for this project was to build a functioning prototype and put it in front of small business owners and start ups to gauge interest in Adziply in the small business marketplace.

User Flows

User flows were designed to gain an understanding of a user's experience, potential pitfalls, points of abandonment/re-engagement, and returning users.

Site Design

The design strategy focused on being as minimalist and straight forward as possible in an effort to not overwhelm or intimidate users.

Other Project Highlights

Prototyping

To keep my remote team memebers informed on the project, several iterations of lo-fi and hi-fi prototypes were shared across the team via InVision prototypes.

Front-end Development

To prevent constant loading times between pages, I built out fully functioning html/css prototypes and coded the dynamic page transitions via jquery.

User Testing

In-person user testing sessions with start-ups and small businesses were conducted to inform UX decisions and gauge interest in the small business marketplace.
