SiteBrain

SiteBrain is an online ad campaign management tool. It was a platform with expanding needs, features, and capabilities and relied on an outdated design infrastructure. I was tasked with building a flexible design system capable of facilitating future growth. I also designed and developed new features and sections of the platform.

Site Mapping

A site map was designed to reflect the current state of SiteBrain to visually represent the scope. I then expanded the site map to include new features and sections.

Site Design

The design needed to have a familiarity with the old system, as to not cause a drastic learning curve for current users who had efficiency with the old system, but also intuitive enough to be user-friendly for future users.
